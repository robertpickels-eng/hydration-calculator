# Hydration · Fueling Calculator

A power-based hydration, osmolality, and event-fueling calculator for endurance
athletes — cycling and running. It estimates energy expenditure from power/pace,
models bottle osmolality and carbohydrate-source splits, projects sweat and
sodium losses, and builds a full event fuel plan with course pacing.

**▶ Live tool:** https://robertpickels-eng.github.io/hydration-calculator/

> ⚠️ **Work in progress.** Calculations, recommended products, and features are
> experimental and may change without notice. **Not medical advice** — see the
> in-app disclaimer before acting on any numbers.

## What it does

- Power-/pace-based energy expenditure and glycogen modeling
- Bottle **osmolality** and carbohydrate-source (glucose / fructose / sucrose) analysis
- **Sweat rate** and **sodium-loss** estimates with acclimatization adjustments
- Event **fuel plan**, course **pacing** engine, plus summary and post-ride debrief
- Runs entirely in your browser — your inputs stay in local storage, and usage
  is measured with privacy-friendly, cookieless analytics (no personal data collected)
